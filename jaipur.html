Jaipur, Rajasthan

Jaipur, the Pink City, is famous for its majestic forts, vibrant bazaars, and rich history. It is a must-visit destination for those seeking royal heritage and colorful culture.

Top Attractions

  • Amber Fort
  • City Palace
  • Hawa Mahal
  • Jantar Mantar
  • Jal Mahal

Activities to Do

  • Explore the forts and palaces
  • Shop for handicrafts in local bazaars
  • Enjoy Rajasthani cuisine
  • Attend cultural shows
