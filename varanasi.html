Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Varanasi is the spiritual heart of India, famous for its ghats, temples, and vibrant rituals along the Ganges. It offers a unique blend of tradition and spirituality.

Top Attractions

  • Kashi Vishwanath Temple
  • Dashashwamedh Ghat
  • Sarnath
  • Assi Ghat
  • Ramnagar Fort

Activities to Do

  • Boat ride on the Ganges
  • Attend Ganga Aarti
  • Explore ancient temples
  • Try local street food
