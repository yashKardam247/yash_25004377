Leh-Ladakh, Jammu & Kashmir

Leh-Ladakh is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, high-altitude passes, and serene monasteries. It is a paradise for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

Top Attractions

  • Pangong Lake
  • Magnetic Hill
  • Thiksey Monastery
  • Nubra Valley
  • Khardung La Pass

Activities to Do

  • Biking and trekking
  • Visit monasteries
  • River rafting
  • Photography
